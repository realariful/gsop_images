Habitat Funds Family Programs with AT&T's Help

Habitat for Humanity ReStore is a non-profit where donated items are sold at a discounted price. The profit from the sales go towards the community. We do this by helping low income families afford housing and much more. In the 2019 fiscal year we have served 729 families throughout our programs.
